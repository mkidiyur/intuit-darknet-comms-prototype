Set up a passkey

A passkey uses your device's biometrics (Face ID, fingerprint, or PIN) to sign you in — no password needed and can't be leaked in a data breach.

  • 1Your device will prompt you to verify with Face ID, Touch ID, or your device PIN.
  • 2Your passkey is securely stored on your device and synced via your iCloud or Google account.
  • 3Next time you sign in, just use your biometric — no password required.
